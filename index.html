Our organic turmeric root powder is naturally sourced from the rich soils of India. A natural, much loved and relied on root, grown and used for hundreds of years in India and throughout Asia.

Organic Turmeric Curcumin is a concentrate of polyphenols from a variety of selected plants, designed to provide the same biological benefits as a regular and varied intake of fruit and vegetables.

Insufficient consumption of fruit and vegetables is associated with a higher risk of developing non-communicable diseases. Organic Turmeric Curcumin has been developed to bridge the nutritional gap between our current diet and WHO recommendations, thanks to its broad spectrum of polyphenols.

Organic Turmeric Curcumin contains over 125 polyphenols from 5 major families of compounds. It is formulated from a variety of selected botanicals, including grape, olive, pomegranate, green tea, grapefruit, blueberry and orange.

The recommended daily dose is 450 mg of Organic Turmeric Curcumin, equivalent to 5 portions of fruit and vegetables. However, a daily intake of 90 to 450 mg provides the polyphenol equivalent of 1 to 5 servings of fruit and vegetables.

Organic Turmeric Curcumin can be used as a dietary supplement or incorporated into food and beverages.

Organic Turmeric Curcumin provides the same biological benefits as a regular and varied intake of fruit and vegetables. It even has a higher antioxidant capacity than 5 portions of fruit and vegetables, helping to maintain biological and physiological balance and protect the metabolism.

Yes, Organic Turmeric Curcumin is non-GMO, gluten-free and suitable for vegans.

How Our Supplement Works:

Each 1440mg daily serving (2 capsules) of our Organic Turmeric contains: 1340mg of Organic Turmeric (670mg per capsule), 80mg of Organic Ginger (40mg per capsule), and 20mg of Organic Black Pepper (10mg per capsule).

Our capsules contain Turmeric powder enriched with Organic Black Pepper (Piperine) and Organic Ginger, to increase the absorption of Curcumin (active ingredient in Turmeric). Furthermore, our supplements are vegan and vegetarian-friendly, contain NO excipients, fillers or binders; we only use ingredients which are sustainably sourced, non-GMO and allergen-free.
